Eggs à la Francaise à la Countess Luann

Descripton

How could you do this to me... question mark... Maybe life is a cabaret and Luann is the star! I won't be taking any etiquette lessons from this Countess, but I will be making these eggs!

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 Large eggs
  • Splash of water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Unsalted butter
  • Suggested toppings: warm tomato sauce, freshly grated parmesan cheese, pesto, truffle oil

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl with a whisk until evenly blended. Add a splash of water and salt and pepper to taste. Beat again briefly until lightly frothy.
  2. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium-low heat. Pour in the beaten eggs.
  3. Stir constantly with a wooden spoon until the eggs are creamy like mashed potatoes and still a little moist, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer to a plate and add desired toppings.
